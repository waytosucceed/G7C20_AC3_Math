Once upon a time, in a vibrant city, a group of friends decided to explore a mysterious abandoned mansion on the outskirts. With flashlights in hand, they ventured into the dark corridors filled with cobwebs and eerie shadows. As they delved deeper into the mansion, they uncovered hidden rooms and secret passageways, unravelling the history of the place.

As the friends left the abandoned mansion, they reflected on their adventure, realising they had learned to identify, classify, and understand the properties of various polygons. Their exploration had transformed into a valuable learning experience, deepening their knowledge of geometric shapes. With newfound confidence, they exited, eager to share their discoveries with others.
